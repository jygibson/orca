Join Us!

This is a monthly membership which will renew on the first of each month. If your membership renews and you do not row, you are still responsible for the payment that month. This is much like a gym membership, you are required to pay your monthly dues, whether you row once or 25 times that month. It is your responsibility to pay the dues within the 5 days of the first month. If your dues are not paid within 5 days of the first month, your membership is subject to suspension and deactivation.

Should you cancel your account and register on another date, you will be subject to another registration fee.

Registration fees will be paid upon registration and then on the 2nd of January each year. The total registration fee will be $120 and be prorated throughout the year. Example: If you register in June, then your registration will only be $60. Then, in January of the following year, you will pay the full amount of $120 annual fee. Annual fees are used to pay for club/boat insurance, as well as equipment maintenance and purchases.
