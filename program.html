Our Programs

We offer rowing programs to meet everyone's needs and levels of experience: Masters (including Novices), Adaptive, and Veterans. In the future, we plan to add a Masters competitive program as well. Learn more about program below. To join ORCA, click on "Join Us" in the navigation menu and complete the form. A membership rep will be in contact soon with more information on payment and waivers.

Master's Program
$50.00/month

The Master's recreational program allows for adults to row together in the morning, usually 5:15am until around 7:00am. College age and over are welcome and boats will be grouped by skill level.

Learn More About Master's Rowing
Veteran's Program
$30.00/month

ORCA works with Freedom Rows to provide discounted memberships and access to rowing for all Veterans through local rowing clubs. All levels of experience are welcome!

Learn More About Freedom Rows
Adaptive Program
$50.00/month

Adaptive rowing (or para-rowing) allows for individuals with all types of physical disabilities to participate in the rowing community. All levels of experience are welcome!

Learn More About Adaptive Rowing
